========
Wikidata
========

This section describes the raw Wikidata_ data products.


    "Wikidata is a free and open knowledge base that can be read and edited by
    both humans and machines.  Wikidata acts as central storage for the structured
    data of its Wikimedia sister projects including Wikipedia, Wikivoyage, Wikisource,
    and others.  Wikidata also provides support to many other sites and services
    beyond just Wikimedia projects! The content of Wikidata is
    `available under a free license`_, exported using standard formats, and
    `can be interlinked to other open data sets`_ on the linked data web."

    -- Wikidata_
